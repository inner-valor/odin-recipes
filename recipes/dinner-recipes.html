pizza burger

wondering whether to get a burger or a pizza well now you can have both with this recipe
what ingredients you need are:

  • pizza dough
  • marinara
  • hamburger patty
  • buns
  • cheese
  • lettuce

Directions:

  1. roll pizza dough to form a disc.
  2. spread marinara on dough.
  3. cook hamburger until halfway done.
  4. put patty on the pizza with cheese and cook.
  5. add lettuce and finish with the bun on top.

Space roasted chicken
