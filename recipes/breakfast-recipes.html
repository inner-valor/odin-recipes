Sour Star Berry Waffles

this recipe requires special star berrys that has been aged for 2 weeks
what ingredients you need are:

  • Sour star berry
  • Permanent milk
  • Waffle batter
  • Moon dust flower

Directions:

  1. soak the sour star berrys in the permanent milk to make them edible.
  2. in a small bowl mix waffle batter with Moon dust flower.
  3. add the berrys and cook for 10 mins in the waffle iron.

Mana muffins

this recipe is for those who are always low on mana at breakfast time. It is difficult recipe but its worth it.
what ingredients you need are:

  • mana berrys
  • rubber stone
  • muffin mix
  • less% permanent milk

Directions:

  1. wash berrys with water and add to the finish muffin mix
  2. mix in the less% permanent milk
  3. add stone to bottom of the muffin tin and pour in batter
  4. bake for 20-23 mins or until toothpick comes out clean

breakfast jelly

this recipe is made by a famous wizard chef who love colorful foods
what ingredients you need are:

  • ranbow gelatin
  • pony dreams
  • Whisk water
  • suger

Directions:

  1. mix gelatin with water
  2. quickly add pony dreams and suger
  3. let it set in the fridge for 2 hours
