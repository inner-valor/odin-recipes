annoying fries

this recipe is one of the most annoying recipe to follow.
what ingredients you need are:

  • potatos that been grown on a underground mountain
  • oil from space
  • dimension salt

Directions:

  1. try to peel and cut the potatos, its almost impossible!
  2. fill a pan halfway with oil
  3. cook potatos before the oil explode for 5 mins
  4. put the cooked fries on a paper towel and pat dry
  5. season the fries with salt

revive bean soup

This recipe is for adventuring parties who are tired of the revival potions
what ingredients you need are:

  • revival bean
  • any broth base
  • mint leaves
  • salt

Directions:

  1. remove revival beans from pods
  2. heat up the broth base
  3. add mint leaves and simmer
  4. add revival beans and cook for 3 days
  5. season with salt
